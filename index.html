Banking

Banking refers to the system of financial institutions and the activities they perform, primarily involving the management and safekeeping of money, facilitating transactions, and providing financial services.

Types of Banks

Central Banks

These are the apex banks in a country, responsible for monetary policy, currency issuance, and regulating the banking system.

Cooperative Banks

These are member-owned financial institutions that operate on a cooperative basis.

Regional Rural Banks

These banks focus on providing banking services to rural areas and agricultural sectors.
